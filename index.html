We are code geeks.

We are web and application developers with a love for creative and innovative interactive processes. With a wide variety of experience in different industries, and a depth of knowledge with various coding software and delivery platforms, we are constantly seeking new technologies while refining existing technologies in the realm of web development. Above all, we are really just geeking out about code.

Elegant

Design is not just something that happens in static pixels. It happens inside any system—simple or complex. Code should never be thrown together haphazardly; it should match a pattern that is reproducible and logical. Code does have guidelines and a “golden mean”; it should be followed.

Never half-ass two things. Whole ass one thing.

Ron Swanson in Parks and Recreation

SEO-Friendly

When a site is put to code, information should be set up in such a way that even a person unfamiliar with code could look at the source and have a good idea of what is going on. In this way, even the most basic of algorithms from search bots will happily aggregate the topics in the page(s) efficiently.

That’s a very nice rendering, Dave. I think you’ve improved a great deal. Can you hold it a bit closer?

HAL 9000 in 2001: A Space Odyssey

Compatible

Audiences these days have a variety of devices from which they might choose to view a site. With the code libraries and information available, sites should be able to display elegantly in almost all media formats. Let’s ensure that the code is compatible.

Complete Web Development

We offer complete web development services. For our clients, this means we sit, learn, and listen from them and then determine the right person to design the site. We procure the hosting or work with the client’s existing hosting company. We do the development, set up the initial version of the site in the content management system, and upload the site. The client is walked through every step.

Hourly Services

In many cases, our team of developers works closely with a leading design boutique or an advertising agency on projects that best fit our core competencies. The developers on our team are available for hourly rates and specific timeframes as needed and have worked in a variety of industries, including pharmaceutical, retail products, professional services, non-profit, and more.

Adirondack Home Services

Adirondack Home Services is a simple, custom WordPress theme designed to take advantage of large, full-color imagery. Our client felt most comfortable editing in the WordPress editor, but still wanted a custom design.

adkhomeservices.com

Daroff Design

The Daroff Design website included a great deal of high-resolution photography in a number of industries, and needed to be separated such that varied audiences could find a related vertical. The client also needed to be able to add imagery easily, which is where our proprietary CMS made things easy.

daroffdesign.com

eDeposit

The eDeposit website required a dual audience appeal in its product offerings. Enter a split-graphic carousel and an attractive (but not too distracting), animated background.

edeposit.com

Geri Radin

The Geri Radin website needed a simple yet elegant way to show off rich photography. Most of the screen needed to be filled with this demonstration of service offering. And our client’s desire to be able to upload pictures of any size easily was also a concern in the implementation of our proprietary CMS.

geriradin.com

Fluehr

The Fluehr website aggregates a great deal of data in the form of obituaries and condolences. Data here is read by various ages of end users, so things needed to be easy to access. In addition, our client needed to spend less time tinkering with difficult content management and be able to quickly and easily update, approve and publish regularly-changing content.

fluehr.com

Inbound Logistics

The Inbound Logistics website presents a tremendous amount of data as well as advertising. Most of the muscle in this site is in the management side where files are imported/exported for use in other media without requiring data entry. The site’s proprietary CMS does a lot of heavy lifting while still being distributed on a load-balanced server group and providing most assets from a CDN.

inboundlogistics.com
